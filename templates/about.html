Our Mission

To give every Indian farmer -from the large farms of Punjab to smallholder plots in Odisha -access to the same precision agriculture intelligence that only large agribusiness could previously afford. We believe data-driven farming is a right, not a privilege.

Our Values

Science-First

Every recommendation is grounded in peer-reviewed agronomic data and validated against real-world crop production datasets from across India.

Farmer-Centered

We speak Hinglish. Our UI works on low-end Android phones. Every design decision starts with: "Does this serve the farmer on the ground?"

Sustainability

Smarter crop selection and disease prevention means less water waste, fewer chemicals, and better land health for future generations.
